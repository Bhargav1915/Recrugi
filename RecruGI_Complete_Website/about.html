About RecruGI

We are a leading training and placement company dedicated to bridging the gap between academic learning and industry expectations. At RecruGI, we nurture talent, provide career guidance, and offer hands-on internship experiences that transform students into job-ready professionals.

Our unique approach to IT training delivers real-world knowledge from expert instructors.

At RecruGI, we build strong foundations in modern IT tools. Our curriculum is curated by industry professionals who understand hiring expectations.

We also support learners with resume building, LinkedIn optimization, and mock interviews to prepare them for success.

Gain live industry exposure through our internship programs and mentorship opportunities.

Our internships are designed to simulate real job roles. Candidates gain exposure to projects, tools, and collaborative work culture.

Our top performers receive experience certificates and are fast-tracked for job placements with hiring partners.

Placement drives are regularly conducted for eligible candidates across multiple sectors.

RecruGI partners with companies across the IT, healthcare, and financial domains for direct placement opportunities.

Our goal is to make sure every trained candidate transitions smoothly into a confident and competent working professional.
