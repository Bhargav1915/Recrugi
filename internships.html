Virtual Internships

RecruGI offers virtual internships for final-year students, freshers, and professionals looking to gain industry exposure. These programs provide real-time tasks, mentor support, and access to our tools and communication systems.

Interns receive hands-on training in technologies like Java, Python, SQL, Azure, AWS, React, and more while working on projects that simulate corporate environments.

Certification & Placement

After completing the internship, every candidate receives a certification and personalized placement support. Top performers are considered for in-house hiring and referred to partner companies for full-time roles.

We focus on building confidence, communication, and collaboration skills to help interns become job-ready with both technical and soft skills polished.
