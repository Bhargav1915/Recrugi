Who We Are

RecruGI is a next-generation career accelerator that connects untapped talent with the right training, tools, and opportunities. Our team of professionals from top industries ensures that every student and job seeker is career-ready through structured programs and personal mentoring.

We bring a modern approach to recruitment and training by integrating real-time tools, soft skill enhancement, and domain-specific guidance. Whether you're a student, graduate, or professional, RecruGI equips you for the future.

Our Project Showcase

Our learners actively work on real-world IT projects that simulate corporate expectations. From frontend development to backend integration and deployment, our platform offers end-to-end project experiences with measurable outcomes.

RecruGI ensures that every individual is not just trained theoretically but has demonstrable experience to showcase in portfolios and interviews, increasing placement confidence significantly.

Internships & Industry Exposure

We offer real-time paid internships for top-performing students to help them gain work experience, confidence, and credibility. Our internship programs are tailored to align with current industry standards and hiring practices.

Interns at RecruGI are mentored directly by industry experts and given access to exclusive projects that make them stand out in recruitment processes. We believe exposure is the best teacher.
